# Welcome to Galley

Galley is a **reader**. It turns any Markdown file into a quiet, beautifully
typeset page — and it will never, ever edit one.

In publishing, a *galley* is the typeset proof of a manuscript: the version you
read, not the version you mark up. That's this app. Your files stay exactly as
they are; they just finally look the way they deserve.

## Make it yours in twenty seconds

1. **Set Galley as your default.** Select any `.md` file in Finder, press
   `⌘I`, choose **Galley** under *Open with*, and click **Change All**.
   From then on, double-click means *read*.
2. **Pick your page.** Click the `Aa` button in the toolbar — Paper or Ink,
   four typefaces, your preferred line width.
3. **Press Space in Finder** on any Markdown file — Galley quietly renders
   your Quick Look previews too.

## What it does

- **Everything renders.** Tables, task lists, footnotes, callouts, syntax-highlighted
  code, Mermaid diagrams, LaTeX math, YAML front matter as a tidy card.
- **It keeps up with your AI.** When an agent is writing a file — a plan, a
  report, a `CLAUDE.md` — Galley follows along live, without stealing your
  scroll position.
- **It's screen-share-proof.** No cursor, no edit mode, no raw syntax. Present
  a document without presenting your editor.
- **It stays out of the way.** `⌘F` to find, `⌘⇧P` to present full screen,
  `⌥⌘E` to export a PDF. The sidebar (`⌃⌘S`) shows the document's outline.

> [!tip]
> Open **Help → Feature Tour** to see every rendering feature on one page.

## What it refuses to do

No editing. No vault. No account. No sync. No subscription. No telemetry.
Galley reads files you open, and that's the whole story.
